Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

We envision a future where every child, regardless of their socioeconomic background, has equal access to quality education, comprehensive healthcare, and the opportunity to live with dignity. A world where no child's potential is limited by circumstances beyond their control, and where communities work together to uplift the most vulnerable among us.

Our Mission

The Mide Foundation is committed to breaking cycles of poverty by providing essential resources, education, and support systems to underprivileged children and their families. Through sustainable programs, community partnerships, and advocacy, we work to create lasting change that empowers individuals and transforms communities for generations to come.

Our Focus Areas

Educational Support

Scholarships, materials, after-school programs, and literacy development to ensure every child can learn and thrive.

Nutritional Support

Meal provision and family education on healthy eating to combat malnutrition and promote development.

Healthcare Access

Vaccinations, check-ups, health campaigns, and emotional support to ensure children grow up healthy.

Family Empowerment

Parent training and social support networks to strengthen families and create stable environments.

Children's Rights

Child protection, awareness campaigns, and policy influence to safeguard children's rights and futures.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our foundation and how you can help.

What programs does The Mide Foundation offer?

We focus on five key areas: Educational Support (scholarships, materials, after-school programs), Nutritional Support (meal provision), Healthcare Access (vaccinations, check-ups), Family Empowerment (parent training), and Children's Rights (protection and advocacy).

How can I donate to support your mission?

You can donate through our WhatsApp link, bank transfer, or via our website. Every contribution directly supports our programs. You can also donate monthly to help us plan and expand our impact. Contact us for bank details and donation options.

How can I volunteer with the foundation?

We welcome volunteers of all backgrounds! Contact us via WhatsApp or email to discuss volunteer opportunities. Whether you have skills in teaching, healthcare, counseling, or simply want to help, we have a role for you. Training and support are provided.

Where does my donation go?

Your donations directly support our programs: scholarships, meal programs, health initiatives, family support, and children's rights advocacy. We maintain transparency and accountability. A detailed breakdown of our finances is available upon request.

Can corporates partner with us?

Absolutely! We welcome corporate partnerships. Companies can sponsor programs, provide in-kind donations, allow employees to volunteer, or support specific initiatives. Let's discuss how we can create impact together. Reach out to us via WhatsApp or email.
